# Astrogram

Astrogram is a free web application for astrophotographers that generates beautiful,
Instagram-ready exposure cards from session metadata.

## What it does
You input your astrophotography session details: telescope, camera, mount, software,
Bortle scale sky quality, and integration times per filter (L, Ha, OIII, SII, R, G, B).
Astrogram renders a professional exposure card with circular progress rings, glassmorphism
UI, and neon accents — ready to share on Instagram or any astrophotography community.

It also includes a plate-solving feature: upload your astrophoto and Astrogram identifies
the region of sky using WCS (World Coordinate System), then overlays labeled annotations
for stars (Tycho-2 catalog), galaxies, nebulae, and clusters (Messier, NGC, IC catalogs).

## Core Features
- Exposure card generator with per-filter integration time tracking (L, Ha, OIII, SII, R, G, B)
- Circular progress ring visualization per filter channel
- Plate solving backed by Astrosolve (astrometry.net-compatible)
- WCS annotation overlay: Messier, NGC, IC, Tycho-2 star catalogs
- Annotation filters: toggle by type (stars, galaxies, nebulae) and magnitude limit
- Bortle scale gradient selector (1–9)
- Custom accent color and background image upload
- High-quality JPEG export

## Tech Stack
Angular 21 (Standalone Components, Signals), TailwindCSS 4, modern-screenshot,
custom TypeScript WCS projection engine (TAN/gnomonic)
